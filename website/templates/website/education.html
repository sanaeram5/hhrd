Education

“Education… is the key to unlocking other human rights.”

Education is a fundamental right of every child in India. Education plays an essential role in a child’s life as it helps them to think and analyse critically and enhance their imagination and thought processes. Education gives exposure Platform for children to achieve their goals & dreams. It plays a key role in shaping and developing a child's emotional and mental strength, preparing them to face any obstacles in their life.

We believe that every child should be able to attend school and complete their education without any discrimination based on caste, gender and socio-economic status.
