HHRD is a trust that focuses on the rights of all children and elderly people and supports under privileged populations to Acknowledge them in our society. HHRD also focuses on the mental wellness of the people of society.

More

To save lives, enable social protection and defeat poverty.
We seek a world of hope that is inclusive and just, where all people live in dignity and security. HHRD embarks itself in providing service to children, especially the unloved and uncared, and help them to achieve their full potential.
